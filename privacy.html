Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026 Last Updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DRVN ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using DRVN, you agree to the practices described here. This Privacy Policy is separate from, and should be read alongside, our Terms and Conditions.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly: - Name and email address (if you sign in / create an account) - Sport preferences, coach name/icon customization - Recovery check-ins (sleep, soreness, energy) - Injury information (body part, severity, pain logs over time) - Weekly feeling/recovery notes you type in - Activity logs (workout type, duration, distance, effort, notes) - Food entries (meals you describe for nutrition estimation) - Photos and videos you upload for form analysis - Messages you send to the AI Coach

Information collected automatically: - App usage data (which features you use, streak activity) - Basic device/browser information necessary for the App to function

We do not knowingly collect precise location, contacts, or payment card details directly (payment information, if applicable, is handled by Apple's App Store or Google Play, not by us directly).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate core features (recovery plans, injury tracking, activity logs, food estimates, AI coaching)
  • Generate AI-assisted responses (coaching feedback, form analysis, food lookups) by sending relevant content to third-party AI providers
  • Personalize the App (coach name/icon, sport preferences)
  • Maintain streaks, badges, and your weekly recap
  • If you use the Social feature, display your name and stats (streak, weekly minutes, average readiness) to other users of the App
  • Improve and maintain the App's functionality and reliability
  • Communicate with you about your account or the App, if you've provided an email

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party AI Providers

Certain features (AI Coach chat, Film Room form analysis, food lookups) send the content you submit — text descriptions, photos, or videos — to third-party AI model providers in order to generate a response. This is necessary for those features to work. We do not control how these providers process data beyond generating the requested response. Do not upload photos or videos containing other identifiable people without their consent.

4. Social / Shared Data

If you use the Social tab, your display name, current streak, weekly training minutes, and average readiness score are stored in a shared location accessible to other users of the App, so that leaderboard and competition features can work. Do not use your full legal name or any sensitive information as your display name if you prefer this information to stay private. You can stop this by not using the Social feature; previously shared entries can be removed by deleting your account (Section 7).

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored primarily on your device. Where cloud storage or a backend is used (in future versions with account sync), we apply reasonable technical safeguards to protect it.
  • No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the App's features, unless you request deletion (see Section 7).

6. Children's Privacy

DRVN is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are between 13 and 18, a parent or guardian should review this Privacy Policy with you. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly. Parents who believe their child has provided us with personal information may contact us using the information in Section 10.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data (available directly in the App under Settings → Delete My Account and Data, or by contacting us)
  • Export your data in a portable format (available in the App under Settings → Export My Data)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, including AI-assisted features
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent

Residents of the EU/EEA, UK, and California (and other jurisdictions with similar laws) may have additional rights under GDPR, CCPA, or equivalent regional laws, including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Third-party AI providers, to generate coaching, analysis, and food-lookup responses (Section 3)
  • Service providers who help us operate the App (e.g., hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Other users, only the specific fields you choose to expose via the Social feature (Section 4)
  • Legal authorities, if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of DRVN, our users, or the public

9. International Data Transfers

If you access DRVN from outside the country where our servers or service providers are located, your information may be transferred internationally. We take reasonable steps to ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.
